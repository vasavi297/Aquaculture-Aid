FAQs

Common questions from farmers
How do I test pond water quality cheaply?
▸

Use low-cost test strips for ammonia, nitrite and pH. Perform weekly checks and aerate if values are outside recommended ranges. Reach out to your local extension officer for lab testing.

Which feeds give good growth at low cost?
▸

Look for feeds with the right protein percentage for the species. Consider mixing local protein sources to cut cost — but always aim for balanced nutrition.

How can I access financial aid?
▸

Check the Resources section for official links to government schemes. Prepare a simple project plan and visit the nearest fisheries office for application support.
