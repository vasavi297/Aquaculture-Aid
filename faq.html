FAQs

Common questions from Users
What is Aquaculture Aid?
▸

Aquaculture Aid is a platform designed to help fish farmers and aquaculture practitioners access resources, community discussions, solutions, and support for common problems in aquaculture.

Who can use Aquaculture Aid?
▸

The platform is open to:

  • Fish farmers
  • Aquaculture professionals
  • Students and researchers
  • NGOs & policy makers
  • Anyone interested in sustainable aquaculture
How does the problem search work?
▸

You can search for problems like “disease,” “climate change,” “training,” “finance,” or “market”, etc. Even if you type wrong spellings or alternatives, the search engine will highlight the closest relevant problem card. For example, typing “climete” will still take you to Climate Change problems.

Can I contribute solutions?
▸

Yes! Our community feature allows users to share solutions, tips, and experiences. Farmers helping farmers is the goal of Aquaculture Aid.

Is Aquaculture Aid free to use?
▸

Yes, it is completely free. Our mission is to make aquaculture resources accessible to everyone.

What kind of problems are listed?
▸

Some major categories include:

  • Disease Outbreaks
  • Climate Change & Weather Impacts
  • Training & Skill Development
  • Financial Support & Insurance
  • Market Access
  • Technology & Innovation
How are solutions verified?
▸

Solutions are community-driven and may come from practitioners, researchers, or NGOs. We encourage multiple perspectives, but users should apply them based on their local context and expert advice.

How can I connect with other farmers?
▸

You can join the Community section, where discussions, experiences, and peer-to-peer support are available.

Can I access Aquaculture Aid offline?
▸

Currently, the platform is web-based. We are working on making offline resources (like PDF guides) available in the future.

How can I suggest new problems or features?
▸

We encourage feedback! You can use the Contact page to suggest new problems, features, or improvements.
