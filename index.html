About Us

Aquaculture Aid is a community service initiative dedicated to supporting and empowering fishers. We understand the challenges fishers face‚Äîunpredictable weather, limited resources, and difficulties accessing markets or equipment. Our goal is to provide practical, low-cost solutions that make a real difference in their lives.

We bridge the gap between fishers and the resources available to them, offering expert advice, modern techniques, government welfare schemes, and training programs to improve productivity and income. Knowledge is empowerment, and through sharing best practices, safety measures, and sustainable fishing methods, we help fishers succeed long-term.

Community collaboration is at the heart of our work. We involve fishers in identifying problems and co-creating solutions, building a network where they can learn, share, and grow together. Our vision is a world where every fisher has the tools and opportunities to thrive.
